Libera is a tool designed to conect users with new content. The informational ecosystem of today's world is constantly shifting and changing. Staying relevant requires constant self-education and a commitment to the cutting edge. Libera's services make keeping up in this informational labyrinth intuitive and enjoyable. Libera does the hard work for your, seeks out new content, and delivers it to you based on your interests. Libera evolves with the informational ecosystem, using Machine Learning techniques to discover new subtopics and categories within specific knowledge domains. Libera is powered by a custom designed webcrawler that moves through the labyrinth for you, constantly running to ensure all passageways are explored, even as they continue to shift and grow.


In it's current state, Libera is only equipped to discover content related to data science, but the story doesn't end here. Flexible programming allows Libera's machine learning models to be easily retrained in new fields and a database optimized for parrelelization means Libera's webcrawling services can run simultaneously on multiple topics without sacrificing efficiency. This is only the beginning of a project that seeks to bridge the gap between user and information and help you stay up-to-date. Feedback allows Libera to better learn your interests and to improve its decision making about what content is and isn't relevant both to a general domain and a specific subtopic. While Libera is still in early development, it is hoped that as it continues to grow it can make more and more intelligent predictions about content and each individual users interest.
